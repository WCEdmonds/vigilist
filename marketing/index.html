VIGILIST 000003

The whole matter lifecycle. One platform.

Ingest anything, keep adding

Relativity-style DAT/OPT productions with AI-assisted field mapping, PST/EML containers with families and threads intact, folders of PDFs and natives. Matters take multiple loads — their productions and your collection, side by side and labeled.

AI review that shows its work

An LLM reads every document against your case criteria and codes it — relevant, key, or not — with confidence, reasoning, and excerpts. Attorneys stay in command with one-click overrides.

Validated like courts expect

Blind control sets, recall and precision with confidence intervals, elusion testing over the discard pile. The statistics that turn "we used AI" into a number a judge accepts.

Search every way

Full-text with phrases, booleans, and wildcards; semantic search when you don't know the words; hit reports with unique-hit counts for negotiating term lists with evidence.

A brief that writes itself

Clustering, theme labels, and an AI case brief with citations that jump straight to the Bates number — your first read of a new production, done before you sit down.

Redactions that can't leak

Reason-coded redactions burned into every produced pixel. Second-pass QC approvals auto-invalidate if a redaction changes afterward — stale sign-offs structurally cannot ship.

Privilege logs, generated

Privilege tags derive withhold and redact-in-part dispositions; the log builds itself from safe metadata only — never from privileged content. Ready to serve as CSV.

Produce like the incumbents

Lock a set for gap-free Bates with families together; render endorsed PDF or single-page TIFF; carve out natives; split volumes by size; deliver Concordance DAT/OPT any platform can load.

Guardrails before the gavel

Locking runs validation first: unapproved redactions, privileged documents going out clean, received documents headed back to their producer. Resolve — or override on the record.

Chain of custody, on demand

Every document tells its story: source, hashes, extraction, tags, decisions, redactions, productions. Matter-level custody reports and an honest exceptions ledger, one click away.

Built for review speed

Keyboard-driven coding, bulk tagging across thousands of documents, queues and batches with statistical QC, and an in-browser viewer for images, text, natives, and media.

Enterprise-ready security

SSO with your firm's identity provider (SAML/OIDC), enforceable per organization; role-based access; an append-only audit trail behind every action.
