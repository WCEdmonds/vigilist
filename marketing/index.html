Big-firm tools, small-firm reality.

Traditional e-discovery platforms are built — and priced — for hundred-lawyer firms: per-user licenses, steep minimums, and a thicket of features you'll never touch. A two-to-five person litigation team needs to do one thing exceptionally well: get through the documents. Vigilist does exactly that, and nothing you don't need.

What's inside

Everything you need to review. Nothing you don't.

Native load-file ingest

Drop in a standard production — DAT and OPT load files, Bates-aware. Vigilist parses the metadata, links the extracted text and natives, and you're ready to review.

Full-text search

Every word of every document, indexed. Find the smoking gun in seconds, not hours.

Document & media viewer

Page images, extracted text, and native video stream right in the browser — no plugins, no downloads.

Keyboard-driven review

Tag, note, and advance with single-key shortcuts. Built for reviewers who live in the keyboard.

Bulk tagging

Code an entire result set at once — by issue, privilege, or responsiveness — across thousands of documents in a single pass.

On the roadmap

Redactions, privilege logs, and AI-assisted review — landing as Vigilist grows with your team.

How it works

From production folder to review in three steps.

  1. 1

    Ingest a production

    Point Vigilist at your production folder. It loads the metadata, extracted text, page images, and native files automatically.

  2. 2

    Search & review

    Full-text search and a fast in-browser viewer get your team through documents quickly.

  3. 3

    Tag, note & export

    Code documents, capture notes, and export your work product when the review is done.
